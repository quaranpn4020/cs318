I got married this summer and my last name has changed. How do I make this change on my student records?

To request a legal name change, please complete the Legal Name Change Form found at http://www.uwec.edu/Registrar/faculty/forms.htm and return the form to Blugold Central, Old Library 1108. If you are a current student but are not on campus and would like to request a name change, you can email Barb Thom. Email requests must be sent using your UWEC account and must contain your maiden name, married name, and student ID. If you are a former student or alumni, send your requests to the Alumni Office.

How do I change my Major, Minor, Degree, or Catalogue Year?

If you have decided that you would like to change your major, minor, degree, or catalogue year go to the Change of Major/Minor web pages and follow the procedures for the department you're planning to change major/minor to.

I want to change my grading option to S/U. How do I do this?

During the first ten days of a classes, students may change the grading basis through MyBlugold CampS. To make this change, go to: MyBlugold CampS > Self Service > Student Center > Enroll, select the class you would like to change the grade basis for, and click the Edit tab above. Using the grading basis drop down arrow select S/U. If this option is not available, the class cannot be taken on a S/U basis. Approval of the Associate/Assistant Dean of the college of your major is required after the first ten days of classes. To obtain approval, a Change of Registration Form (also known as the Drop/Add Form) should be completed and returned to Blugold Central, Old Library 1108.
